📖 Weapon 1: Rise Before Ruin

A boy with no ambition, no discipline, and no direction walks into a silent forest—unaware that this single step will change not only his life, but the balance of the world itself. Satyapal has always lived without purpose, drifting through life without responsibility, until fate chooses him for something far greater than he ever imagined.

Chosen as a Satya Yoddha, he becomes one of three warriors destined to confront a rising force linked to the ancient power of Ravana. But this is not a tale of heroes born strong. It is a story of ordinary individuals forced into extraordinary roles.

Along the way, he meets Dharma and Sree—two warriors shaped by pain, belief, and survival. Together, they uncover a dangerous truth: religion is being corrupted, power is being misused, and truth is being silenced.

As the war escalates, sacrifices become inevitable. In a final battle in Lanka, everything is pushed to its limit—and when truth itself falls, something greater rises.
