Weapon 1

A boy with no purpose. A world silently breaking. A truth powerful enough to change everything. Satyapal lives a life without direction, drifting through days without meaning or responsibility. But everything changes when he unknowingly walks into a silent forest—an event that pulls him into a hidden war far greater than he could ever imagine. Chosen as a Satya Yoddha, he becomes one of three warriors destined to protect the balance of the world against a rising force tied to the ancient power of Ravana. But this is not a story of heroes born strong—it is about ordinary people forced into extraordinary situations. Along his journey, he meets Dharma, a man who has lost faith but not courage, and Sree, a silent and deadly warrior who acts without fear. Together, they uncover a dark truth: religion is being corrupted, power is being misused, and truth itself is being silenced. As their journey deepens, battles grow intense, sacrifices become inevitable, and trust is constantly tested. The final war leads them to Lanka, where everything—strength, belief, loyalty, and truth—is pushed to its limits. And when it feels like truth has fallen… something greater rises. This is not just a story of war—it is a story of transformation, belief, sacrifice, and the power of truth in a broken world.

Empire Of Football

₹470

Football is more than just a sport—it is emotion, identity, history, and power. Empire Of Football takes you through the journey of the world’s most popular game, from its humble beginnings to its rise as a global empire that influences millions. At the center of this story lies one of the greatest rivalries in history: FC Barcelona vs Real Madrid. These are not just clubs—they represent ideologies. Barcelona stands for unity, culture, and beautiful football built on teamwork. Real Madrid stands for dominance, ambition, and an unstoppable winning mentality. Through legendary players, iconic matches, and unforgettable moments, the book explores how these two giants didn’t just compete—they shaped football itself. But this is not just about history. It dives into mindset—the pressure, discipline, failures, and determination required to succeed at the highest level. Football becomes a reflection of life, where every decision matters, every moment counts, and only those with the strongest mentality rise to greatness. Empire Of Football is not just about the game—it is about legacy, rivalry, and building your own empire.
